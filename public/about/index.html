About

I’m a Technology Portfolio Director, founder, and builder focused on creating tools that empower people in the digital age. My work, both professional and personal, is guided by a few core beliefs.

My Approach #

  • The Expert Generalist: I combine deep expertise in technology delivery with broad knowledge across product, strategy, execution, and AI to connect the dots others miss.

  • Digital Sovereignty: I believe we need to own our digital lives. This principle drives my hardware venture and is why this website is built on technology I control.

  • The Portfolio Approach: I apply portfolio theory to life, balancing a stable day job with high-risk, high-reward personal ventures. This creates resilience and allows ideas to cross-pollinate.

  • Build in Public & Ship Imperfectly: I share the journey, not just the outcome. Getting real-world feedback is more valuable than waiting for perfection—it’s the agile mindset in practice.

  • AI-Assisted Everything: I use AI as a creative partner and a force multiplier to code, write, and learn faster, augmenting my ability to build and create.

My Work in Action #

These beliefs aren’t just theoretical; they’re how I operate.

  • By Day, I apply them in my role as a Technology Portfolio Director, leading large-scale product delivery and AI transformation.
  • By Night, I use them to build my own ventures, like MyDigitAlly, an AI-powered platform for parents.

This site is my digital garden where I document what I’m learning. If you’re new here, the best places to start are my Now page or my latest Notes.
