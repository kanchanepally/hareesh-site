Week 31 #Weeknotes: Aug 5-9, 2024

August 12, 2024

Work front #

During my last published #weeknotes, I mentioned about doing a 360-degree risk review of my portfolio of programmes and coming up with a list ahead of a leadership offsite. I feel I ought to share that here.

  • Confusion amongst cross-functional delivery vs domain-based organisation structure.

  • Confusion between iterative model of delivery vs stage-gates way of monitoring/governance

  • Lack of end-to-end focus on product delivery from all the disciplines involves.

  • Lack of aligned ways of working for the domain teams (an off-shoot of the second item above)

We discussed these issues and how they are slowing us down and came up with a set of actions to fix that. Coincidentally, Scott Ambler (co-creator of Discipline Agile) published an article just today that I thought summarises some of the causes of the above issues. I could hug him for writing this up! And I am sure most of the #TPMs can relate.

Personal front #

I am smashing through the fat – literally – and my total diet replacement is starting to yield (or should I say “shedding”) the results. I have lost 1.5 stone (10kg) in 1 month. And while it’s plateauing, I hope that it will get me back to a health state. I am really looking forward to writing up the phenomenology of the diet replacement ;-p. I am sure you’re eagerly looking forward to reading that thrilling read.

Summer has started, and we’re having fun. Enjoying the days: whether the sun’s out or not. Camping, sailing, watching movies, joining in village parades, and the lot.
