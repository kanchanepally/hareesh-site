Stealth Hardware Project

November 1, 2024

Digital Sovereignty for Everyone #

An early-stage hardware venture focused on true digital sovereignty - giving individuals complete ownership and control of their digital lives.

The Vision #

In a world where our data is constantly extracted, analyzed, and monetized by corporations and governments, we’ve lost agency over our digital selves. Current “privacy” solutions are bandaids on a fundamentally extractive system.

This project explores hardware-based approaches to genuine digital autonomy - infrastructure that puts individuals back in control.

Current Status #

Early exploration phase. Researching:

  • Hardware architecture approaches
  • Digital sovereignty protocols
  • User needs and pain points
  • Manufacturing and distribution models

Timeline: Aiming for public reveal in early 2026

Why Hardware? #

Software solutions can always be compromised, backdoored, or changed by external parties. Hardware provides a foundation for sovereignty that users can physically control and verify.

Philosophy #

This aligns with my broader work on digital empowerment. Just as MyDigitAlly gives parents agency through knowledge, this project aims to give individuals agency through infrastructure.

Updates will appear here as the project develops. If you’re interested in digital sovereignty, follow along on /notes where I share thinking in this space.
