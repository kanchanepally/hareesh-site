Stealth Hardware Project

Digital Sovereignty for Everyone

An early-stage hardware venture focused on true digital sovereignty - giving individuals complete ownership and control of their digital lives.

The Vision

In a world where our data is constantly extracted, analyzed, and monetized by corporations and governments, we’ve lost agency over our digital selves. Current “privacy” solutions are bandaids on a fundamentally extractive system.

This project explores hardware-based approaches to genuine digital autonomy - infrastructure that puts individuals back in control.

November 1, 2024

MyDigitAlly

Empowering Parents in the Digital Age

MyDigitAlly is a freemium edtech startup that helps non-tech parents confidently navigate their kids’ digital world.

The Problem

Parents today face an impossible challenge: they need to guide their children through a digital landscape they don’t fully understand themselves. The pace of technological change means even tech-savvy parents struggle to keep up with new platforms, risks, and opportunities.

Most resources are either too technical, too judgmental, or too time-consuming for busy parents.

November 1, 2024
